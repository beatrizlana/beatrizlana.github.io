ShoeWorks is in the Irish marketplace since 2002 and since then selling high quality products to customers. We always regard to offer the best and exclusive products, because we want ShoeWorks's customers to be unique and fashion at the same time.

In 2015, we are starting an online service in order to provide more possibilities, easiness and comfort to our customers. Now it's possible to buy our products online and just go to the store to pick them. Moreover, if the size doesn't fit or any other issue with the product, the customer can change it by another one available in the store in that day or we can order another product from our supplier, so it will be in our store up to 10 working days. Customers satisfaction is our first and most important motivation on ShoeWorks!
